Our mission

We invite you to explore the Kunashir Island with its active volcanoes, thermal springs, hot lakes, and wild nature. Walk, hike, climb volcanoes, explore unique geological sites, observe numerous species of animals, take a cruise around the islands and taste delicious, one of its kind seafood – all this during one trip, the Kuril Islands Adventure!
